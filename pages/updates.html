Meeting With Prof. Kevin Ryan

Professor Kevin Ryan discussed the possibility of enhancing current output in our design through the use of a noninverting op-amp at the expense of dampening the voltage slightly. After some review, he recommended using the LM741 chip to provide a potential output of 80 - 100 mA.
