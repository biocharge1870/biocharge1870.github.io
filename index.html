Harness The Power Of Piezoelectricity!

Our teams innovative design utilizes the piezoelectric effect to generate power with every step you take. Enjoy a faster mode of travel with an endless supply of energy for all your charging needs.

What Is The Piezoelectric Effect?

Certain materials can have their electrons artificially pulled in one direction by applying a strong electic field to the material. This electron poling allows us to produce an electric charge by agitating the material either through bending or axial strain. Our design approach takes advantage of this by straining the piezoelectric material with every single step you take generating power you can use! The more strain, the more power!

Our Design

Our team wanted to build a device that was both entertaining to use as well as efficient in its power generation. We settled on this binding based system as it can enhance the mobility of our users, creating a novel and quick way to move around while amplifying the strain induced on the piezoelectric materials in use. Along with an attached induction coil generator acting as a primary current source, the design harvests energy in an efficient manner while making use comfortable and fun!
